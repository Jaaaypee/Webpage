Our services

Zoo Tour

Come and see the animals we conserve with our experienced tour guide. Learn more fun facts about them and how to survive in the wild if ever that you will encounter the animals in the wild.

Zooperience

Have a full blown encounter with some of the animals in our zoo. You can feed and pet some of them. One thing to remember everything has a limit for some of the animals requires rest and space.

Zootopia

Enjoy your stay on our zoo with some thrilling games and rides as well as farms to buy our sustainable and eco-friendly products.

Meet Our Pals

Lion

The African Lion is admired for centuries due to their symbol of courage and strength. Here at Wonder Zoo the lions are being taken care of far from poachers that can cause them to extinction.

Giraffe

Giraffe is known for being the world’s tallest mammals, a big thanks to their long legs and neck . They might be 6 feet taller than human, but they are pretty protective and playful, yet they can remain elegant. Here at Wonder Zoo, they are loved and conserve for future generations.

Tiger

Tigers are known for their red-orange pattern stripes. They also gained the recognition of largest cat in the world. They are pretty good hunters and can bring down deer and antelopes during their hunt. Here in Wonder Zoo they enjoy their life without restrictions and worry that hunters might take them down.

King Cobra

The famous venomous snake is here in the Wonder Zoo in order to conserve their species and make the public knowledgeable about their characteristics.

Zebra

The famous patterned mammal are the most sturdy and spirited animals. They are known for being willful, playful, resilient, and vulnerable. Other than that, they have excellent hearing and eyesight that can speed up to 35 miles per hour. Here at Wonder Zoo, they are being taken care of.
